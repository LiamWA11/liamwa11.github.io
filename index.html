Levels of Expertise

Below, there are various pages on our site that will provide you with all of the information needed to start playing Tennis at ANY level, being Local, State or National. There is also some information, under national, about international tennis competitons.

Local

Welcome, on this page you can find all of the information needed to start playing Tennis at your local club. We provide information for ALL Australian capital cities, along with various other sites that offer information about other Australian cities.

State

Hello, on this page you can find all of the information that you will need to start, continue playing tennis at a state level. This page will provide information on competitions, events adn clubs in all Australian states & territories.

National

You must be pretty good, this is for the elite. On this page you can find information on all of the major tennis tournaments happening in Australia, and around the world. Good luck on your path to glory!
