    Who Am I

    A student, is the short answer, to who I am...
    I am currently a Year 10 student in Australia, studying Media & Digital Technology. I enjoy pretty much everything technology, which is one of the reasons why I decided to create this website, to share my love of technology with the world. I am currently learning C#, by creating simple retro games, and gradually increasing the difficulty of the game that I try to program. I also absolutely love Math & Science, and currently aspire to be a Pediatric Surgeon in the future. I also enjoy 3D printing, and got a version of the Prusa i3 for christmas, so look out for 3D printing things in the future.
