Queensland Tennis Centre


Tennis Queensland is the governing body of tennis in Queensland and forms a link in the chain of member associations responsible to Tennis Australia, the governing body of tennis in Australia. Tennis Queensland promotes and facilitates participation in tennis at all levels and strives to steer the growth of the sport for the present and future. The key to the enduring success of tennis is that it is a fun, healthy, social activity - a game that can be played at any level for a lifetime. All you need is a ball, a court, a hitting partner and a racquet.
