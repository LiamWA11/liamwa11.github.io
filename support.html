    Why Must I Give You Money

    You are under no obligation to give me money
    This being said, receiving money from a side project such as this site, provides me with an incentive to continue development of both the products and services featured on this site. It also allows me to spend more time doing something that I enjoy, which is a plus. Dontating money to me is also an invaluable method of customer feedback, which allows me to understand which products and services are most popular. Money can also lead to the expansion of this website, and more specifically my tutoring service, to encompass more subjects, and to have more time devoted to them, which creates a higher quality service for you guys.

    Direct Donation

    Have you seen something that you like, or are just in a generous mood, then this is the place for you. Here you are able to give me as much or as little money as you want, instantly. There are no 'lock-in contracts', and this is a one time transaction. You can submit a message with the donation, and can be assured that you will receive an e-mail from me in response to your message.
