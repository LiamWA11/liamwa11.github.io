    Tutoring, You say?

    This project stems from the apparent need, of many of my classmates, to get extra help with the projects and work that is being done in class. I am a great student, straight A's on every subject, and an amazing ability to research and learn, or be able to give others the resources to learn, very quickly. I decided that I would put this talent to good use, by offering a tutoring service.

    The two essential (core) subjects in which I am most comfortable in are Math & Science, which is why you can access their pages at the top of this page. This being said, I am also very adept in most other areas of study, which are covered by the General category. If I begin to get a large number of requests for a specific subject not listed, it may find it's way to this page...


    Methods of Tutoring

    At the moment, I am going to be launching two methods of tutoring...
    Method 1 will be example problems. You will be able to submit a photo, or textual explanation of any problem, and I will reply with a step-by-step solution to a SIMILAR problem. This service will be free, and ad-support (so a lot more ads on the submission page). This method of tutoring should suffice for most people, trying to understand a particularly complicated problem, or just needing help with completing their homework.

    Method 2 of tutoring will be a customised lesson with various video & textual references. This type of tutoring will be a paid solution, and is aimed at those who need help with a concept in a specific subject, or are struggling to grasp a specific idea. Each lesson will include numerous example problems, step-by-step guides, and near instant help with any problems on most days. You can also request a specific type or method of explanation, to further enable you to grasp the concept.
